Monitor your health daily with Achilles Health

App APK

Monitoring one's health daily is a difficult task especially during the time of a pandemic. Achilles Health is a simple and effective solution to this problem
  1. Measure Respiratory Rate:- In order to masure respiratory rate the user must place his/her phone on his/her chest lying in supine position. It measures user's Respiratory wth the help of IMU sensors of the phone.
  2. Measure Heart Rate:- In order to masure heart rate the user must place his/her index finger on rear camera of phone. The rear camera will turn on torch light of the phone and try to record a video of blood pumping through the veins or arteries of finger. The app will process on the video recorded on the phone and try to predict heart rate of the user.
  3. Report Symptoms:- In this feature the user can measure his/her daily symptoms and save them into a database.
